Shiksha Valley School

Redefining Education

A Learning Community of motivated students and teachers engaged in active learning through the best use of modern technology. The world is progressing at a frenetic pace with India moving towards digital living and smarter technologies.

WHY svs?

”At Shiksha Valley School is an inception of knowledge both tactical and operational provided for education and diligence!”

Holistic Education

We offer an unparalleled educational experience with innovative teaching, a nurturing environment, and a curriculum that fosters intellectual, emotional, and social growth through joyful, interactive learning.

Equipping Students for Success

We equip students with critical thinking, communication, and collaboration skills in a secure, nurturing environment for academic, social, and emotional growth.

Fostering Student Excellence

Our educators nurture unique talents, build confidence, and encourage self-expression, while global connectivity enhances learning.

Inspiring Tomorrow's Leaders

Our modern campus supports a comprehensive educational experience preparing students for future challenges, empowering them to reach their fullest potential.

CO-CHAIRMAN’S MESSAGE

“Any idea of development passes through stages of many dimensions, decisions and alternatives to choose from. My entourage on the Shiksha Valley School was to help my father achieve his dream while making important decisions to favour the setting up one of the finest school of the region.

-Shri Hemanta Kumar Gogoi

Founder Co-Chairman

MANAGING DIRECTOR’S MESSAGE

I had always dreamt of building a school which lays focus on quality education and sheer excellence in nurturing the creativity of our children.

-Shri Pulin Chandra Gogoi

Founder Managing Director

JOINT MANAGING DIRECTOR’S MESSAGE

DIRECTOR’S MESSAGE

We have holistic approach for multi dimensional growth of the learners.
